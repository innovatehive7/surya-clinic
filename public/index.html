About Our Clinic

Our unique approach combines ancient Ayurvedic wisdom with modern dental practices to provide comprehensive healthcare for the entire family.

Our Story

Founded by a husband and wife team of healthcare professionals, Surya Clinic was born from the vision of offering holistic healthcare that addresses both traditional and modern medical needs.

Our facility combines the tranquil healing environment needed for Ayurvedic treatments with the state-of-the-art equipment required for advanced dental procedures. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms.

Meet Our Doctors

Our experienced husband and wife team brings together expertise in Ayurvedic medicine and modern dental science.

Ayurvedic Specialist

Dr. Rahul Sharma

B.A.M.S, MD (Ayurveda)

With over 15 years of experience in Ayurvedic medicine, Dr. Rahul specializes in holistic treatments that address the root cause of health issues. His approach combines traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern medical knowledge.

  • Graduated from Pune Ayurvedic College
  • Certified in Panchakarma Therapy
  • Specializes in digestive disorders
  • Expert in herbal formulations
  • Speaks English, Hindi, Marathi
Dental Specialist

Dr. Priya Sharma

BDS, MDS (Orthodontics)

Dr. Priya brings 12 years of experience in dental care with expertise in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. She is passionate about creating beautiful, healthy smiles while ensuring patient comfort.

  • Graduated from Dental College of Pune
  • Specialization in Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Expert in dental implants
  • Certified Invisalign provider
  • Speaks English, Hindi, Marathi
